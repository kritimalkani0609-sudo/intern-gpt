      Registration Fee Scam

      Example Message:

      "Congratulations! Pay ‚Çπ500 registration fee to secure your internship."

      Red Flags:
      • Asking for upfront payment
      • Pressure to pay immediately
      • No official company verification
      What to Do:

      Legitimate internships never ask for registration fees. Report and block immediately.

      üìÑ

      Fake Offer Letter Scam

      Example Message:

      "Your offer letter is ready. Download from this link to proceed."

      Red Flags:
      • Suspicious download links
      • No interview process
      • Generic offer letter
      What to Do:

      Verify company website, check LinkedIn, and never download from untrusted sources.

      üßë‚Äçüíª

      Unpaid Task Scam

      Example Message:

      "Complete this project task first. We'll evaluate and then offer the internship."

      Red Flags:
      • Asking for free work upfront
      • No guarantee of internship
      • Complex projects without pay
      What to Do:

      Legitimate companies don't require unpaid work before hiring. Request proper contract.

      üîó

      Telegram / WhatsApp Scam

      Example Message:

      "Join our Telegram group for internship updates. Limited slots available!"

      Red Flags:
      • Only uses messaging apps
      • No official company email
      • Urgent "limited slots" pressure
      What to Do:

      Verify through official company channels. Legitimate companies use professional email.

      Student Safety Checklist

      Use this checklist before accepting any internship offer

      Company has official website
      Verified LinkedIn page
      No upfront payment required
      Proper offer letter provided
      Official email domain (@company.com)
      Clear job description and role
      Professional interview process
      No urgent pressure tactics
